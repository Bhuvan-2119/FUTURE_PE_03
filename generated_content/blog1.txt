H1: What is PPE Detection Using AI?

Introduction:
Ensuring workers wear proper safety gear is critical in construction. PPE detection using AI helps automate this process and reduces risks significantly.

H2: What is PPE Detection?
PPE detection is a technology that uses AI to identify whether workers are wearing safety equipment like helmets, gloves, and reflective jackets.

H2: How AI Detects PPE
- Cameras capture real-time footage
- AI models analyze images
- Alerts are generated for violations

H2: Benefits of PPE Detection
- Prevents workplace injuries
- Ensures safety compliance
- Reduces manual supervision

H2: Use Cases in Construction
- Helmet detection alerts
- Safety vest compliance checks
- Entry restriction for unsafe workers

H2: How It Connects to AI Safety Systems
PPE detection is a core part of AI-based construction safety systems, helping companies automate safety monitoring.

H2: Conclusion
AI-powered PPE detection improves safety standards and reduces accidents effectively.
